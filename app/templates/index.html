Generation Coders

Making technology accessible to all generations

Who we are:

We are a team of high school students from Bergen County Academies dedicated to spreading Computer Science skills throughout the state.

Our mission is to be able to provide a learning opportunity in Computer Science across the state for all who are interested regardless of skill, age, or background!

Learn More

Education

We believe that education is the key to success: our primary goal is to make education as accessible as possible, and we strive to be the best teachers that we can be in all of our workshops and programs.

Community

We strive to create the best learning environment possible and we believe that the best way to learn is through community and partnership!

Motivation

We want to make sure that you'll have the motivation, inspiration, and resources to continue programming and achieve success :)
